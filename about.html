Hello! I'm Ashvin and I'm a current PhD student at Imperial College London on the AI for Healthcare CDT and part of the SPIKE group under Professor Alessandra Russo. My research is focused on trying to diagnosis multiple cancers from the EHR data using machine learning as well as building a computational model of the NICE guidelines for cancer management.

The PhD is a great opportunity to learn and grow as a researcher and I've set this blog up to share my thoughts and learnings on the projects I'm working, life as a PhD student and lessons from the modules I am assisting with.
