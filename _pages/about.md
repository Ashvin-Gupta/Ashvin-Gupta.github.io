PhD at Imperial College London on the [AI for Healthcare](https://ai4health.io/organisation/) programme. Part of the Structured and Probabilistic Intelligent Knowledge Engineering ([SPIKE](https://spike.doc.ic.ac.uk/)) group run by Professor Alessandra Russo. My research focuses on assisting primary care clinicians to diagnose cancers earlier through developing deep learning based models for cancer prediction and building clinical decision support systems for cancer management. 

This website will consist of insights from my research, topics related to Machine Learning and and life as a PhD student as well as a bit of travel and cricket analysis.
